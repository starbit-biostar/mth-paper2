## Hypothesis for Paper 2
Ancient civilizations that built astronomical monuments may have been tracking the same cycles that MTH detects in reproductive timing:
1. Lunar/eclipse cycles (Metonic, Saros) → transgenerational window
2. Jupiter-Saturn conjunctions → the "60-year" anchor
